Our team


 Ludicartes is a business in the Stanislas Center of JA Quebec. Our card game company is made up of 10 students of the College Stanislas of Montreal. We are all proud to participate in Junior Achievement Québec and would like to share our experiences.

 Our goal is to provide entertainment for family and friends at a very reasonable price. We value creativity, quality and fun.

 Get to know us by reading a short text written by each member of the team about their experience at Ludicartes.

Mathias Andrivon
