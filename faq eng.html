Frequently Asked Questions


 If you are here, that means you are asking yourself about our product. Find answers to common questions under this. If you still have questions after that, don't hesitate to contact us using the links at the bottom of the page.

How are your card games made?

The whole process is done by our team. We start by digitally drawing the card. Then, we can print it. We cut, laminate, then cut again. We check that the good amount of cards is in each pack before declaring the product to be done.


How do I know how to play your card games?

An instruction card is included in each pack of cards. Unfortunately, that card is only in French. If you want a translation for the rules, please contact us directly.


What is included in each pack of cards?

Each pack comes with a bag for holding the cards. For 10/10, there are 54 playing cards and 1 instruction card. For Rupture, there are 53 unique playing cards, each having a different drawing on them, and 1 instrucion card.


Who are you?

We are a dozen of Secondary 5 students who make up the student business Ludicartes. If you want to learn more, please follow this link.


How much does a pack of cards cost?

Including taxes, a pack of cards costs 10$.


How can I buy a pack of cards?

To buy a pack, you will have to contact us so we can arrange a meeting in Montreal. Indeed, JA regulations force us to exchange money and product at the same time. For this reason, we are also not allowed to send packs by mail or accept online purchases.


How can I support you?

You can support us by talking about Ludicartes to your acquaintances and by following us on social media. Of course, you can also buy our card games, but even merely opening this website help us grow.
